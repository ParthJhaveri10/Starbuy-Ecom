ABOUT US

Founded at the start of 2022 and based in Mumbai, India, Starbuy is a trusted community marketplace for people and businesses to list, discover, and sell all kinds of products - online, from a mobile phone, or a tablet.

Whether its clothes, groceries or electronics, Starbuy connects the customer to the seller, for the sale of a product, from any part of the world and with world-class customer service and a growing community of users. Starbuy is the easiest way for people to monetize and showcase their products to an audience of millions willing to buy.

MISSION

We aim to develop technologies to better the communication of the customer and the seller so that the relationships can be boosted. The customer experience and boosting all of the services on our website is of high importance to us. Our mission is to make Starbuy one of the most reliable e-commerce platforms.

VISION

Our vision is to be earth's most customer centric company; to build a place where people can come to find and discover anything they might want to buy online. We pioneer communities built on commerce, sustained by trust, and inspired by opportunity and bring together millions of people every day.

ACHIEVEMENTS

In less than a year, we have 3 million satisfied customers who keep coming back to us everytime they are in need. Our sales have increased tenfold over the past 6 months and we are grateful to our customers. Starbuy currently stands as one of the best upcoming e-commerce websites in the world.
